Refund Policy

PulseCal Refund Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Overview

At PulseCal, we strive to provide exceptional service to our users. This refund policy outlines the circumstances under which refunds may be issued for our services and subscription plans.

2. Service Availability

PulseCal provides a healthcare appointment management platform. Our core services include:

  • Appointment scheduling and management
  • Patient and doctor profiles
  • Telemedicine session support
  • Medical records management
  • Payment processing for healthcare services

3. Subscription Refunds

Premium Subscription Plans

For premium subscription plans, the following refund terms apply:

  • 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee: New subscribers may request a full refund within 30 days of initial purchase
  • Pro-rated Refunds: After 30 days, refunds are calculated on a pro-rated basis for unused months
  • Annual Plans: 50% refund available for annual plans cancelled within 6 months

4. Payment Processing Fees

For healthcare service payments processed through our platform:

  • Refunds are subject to the healthcare provider's cancellation policy
  • Processing fees (2-3%) are non-refundable
  • Refund processing time: 5-10 business days
  • Original payment method will be credited

5. Non-Refundable Items

The following items are non-refundable:

  • Processing fees and transaction costs
  • Completed telemedicine sessions
  • Consultation fees for completed appointments
  • Custom development or integration services
  • Training sessions and onboarding services

6. Cancellation Policy

Appointment Cancellations
  • 24+ hours notice: Full refund or reschedule
  • 2-24 hours notice: 50% refund or reschedule
  • Less than 2 hours: No refund, reschedule subject to provider policy
  • No-show: No refund, full charge applies

7. Technical Issues

If you experience technical issues that prevent service usage:

  • Report the issue within 48 hours of occurrence
  • We will investigate and provide appropriate compensation
  • Service credits may be offered in lieu of refunds
  • Extended outages may qualify for subscription extensions

8. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact our support team at support@pulsecal.com
  2. Include your account information and reason for refund
  3. Provide relevant documentation (receipts, screenshots)
  4. Allow 3-5 business days for review
  5. We will notify you of the decision via email

9. Refund Processing

Once approved, refunds will be processed as follows:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: 5-10 business days
  • Bank Transfers: 3-7 business days
  • Digital Wallets: 1-3 business days
  • Service Credits: Immediate

10. Dispute Resolution

If you disagree with a refund decision:

  • Submit a formal appeal within 30 days
  • Include additional supporting documentation
  • Our management team will review within 10 business days
  • Final decision will be communicated in writing

11. Contact Information

For refund-related inquiries:

  • Email: refunds@pulsecal.com
  • Phone: 1-800-PULSECAL
  • Support Hours: Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 6 PM EST
  • Emergency: support@pulsecal.com (24/7)
Our Commitment: We are committed to fair and transparent refund practices. Your satisfaction is our priority.
