About PulseCal

PulseCal is a comprehensive appointment management system designed for clinics, hospitals, doctors, and patients. It enables seamless scheduling, real-time doctor availability, and secure patient data management for healthcare organizations of all sizes.

  • Multi-organization support (clinics & hospitals)
  • Role-based dashboards for doctors, patients, and receptionists
  • Easy appointment booking and management
  • Exportable patient data per organization
  • Modern, secure, and user-friendly interface

For more information, contact your clinic or hospital administrator.
